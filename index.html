📋 FAQ

How do I cancel my booking? ▾
Go to Dashboard → Active Bookings → Cancel. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method. Cancellations within 30 minutes of booking time are non-refundable.
What is the refund process? ▾
Refunds are issued to the original payment method. UPI refunds take 1–2 days, card refunds take 3–5 days, and wallet refunds are instant. For payment failures, auto-refund is initiated within 24 hours.
Can I extend my parking duration? ▾
Yes! Go to Dashboard → Active Bookings → Extend. You can extend in 1-hour increments. Extension charges are applied at the current rate (including peak surge if applicable).
How does peak pricing work? ▾
Peak pricing applies between 6PM and 10PM daily, adding a 25% surge to base rates. Bike: ₹25/hr, Car: ₹62.50/hr, EV: ₹50/hr during peak hours.
What happens if I overstay? ▾
You'll receive a push notification 10 minutes before expiry. Overstay charges are billed at the hourly rate automatically. You can pay at the exit gate or extend via the app.
